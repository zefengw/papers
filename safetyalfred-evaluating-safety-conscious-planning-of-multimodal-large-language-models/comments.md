# Comments

## Strengths
- Moves safety eval from static QA to embodied corrective behavior.
- Better mirrors deployment risks for household/service robots.
- Useful benchmark pattern for multimodal agent safety audits.

## Limitations
- Sim-to-real transfer remains open.
- Mitigation success depends heavily on planner-controller interfaces.
- Hazard taxonomy scope can bias outcomes.

## Extension ideas
- Add temporal hazard escalation and delayed effects.
- Include human override / intervention channels.
- Evaluate robustness under noisy or missing metadata.
