# Critical Notes — Language Models are Few-Shot Learners

## Strengths
- Demonstrated that scale itself can induce broad in-context capabilities.
- Shifted evaluation culture toward prompt-based, task-agnostic assessment.

## Limitations
- Benchmark contamination risk and prompt sensitivity were already visible.
- Few-shot performance remained uneven for compositional reasoning and robust factuality.

## Potential extensions
- Replace lexical similarity with embedding-based nearest-neighbor retrieval.
- Add calibration/confidence scoring for abstention decisions.
- Expand to few-shot chain-of-thought style demonstrations.
